Deploy & Test

We test the lessons in the field in India, China, and Kenya for cultural appropriateness and effectiveness. We then solve the logistical problems and buying the materials locally or shipping them. We and create benchmarks to measure the effectiveness and prevent fraud and abuse.

Scale Out

We take the program to the kids in multiple countries in the world. We partner with other NGOs and use their existing on the ground infrastructure to deploy the program and monitor the results.

We help kids
Dream of a better future

We help kids visualize themselves in a professional role. We empower them to think beyond the limits of their current physical and economic status and see a brighter future.
We inspire kids to dream. And give them the tools to get closer to making that come to reality.

I want to become a doctor.

11 year old girl, India.

We help kids solve logical problems.


How do you draw a two-story house?
When a child is confronted and solves this logical problem he or she will never forget this lesson.
Our program push the kids explore the boundaries of reasoning and perceive the world around them in in detail.

We help kids invent the future

We teach kids to create something new to solves a problem. We team them how to express themselves and communicate their creation to other people. Story telling and selling your ideas is a critical step along the path of becoming a successful professional.
