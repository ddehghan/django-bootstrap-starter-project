Our Mission

Education is the means that empowers children to realize their own dreams and potential. However, millions of children around the world lack basic educational resources.

Draw The Future Foundation helps fill that gap by creating effective, educational programs that nurture childrenâ€™s creativity and encourage them to dream big.

In many developing counties schools are a dry and rigid place where children are forced to memorize and recite blindly. We aim to change that by helping kids have fun while learning.

The first project along this journey is to bring fun, creativity and empowerment to children through drawing. We provided drawing supplies and ask the kids to draw a picture of their family or their future job. With the help of volunteers and local teachers we nudge the kids to envision a better life for themselves and their family and increase their desire to stay in school and lean.
